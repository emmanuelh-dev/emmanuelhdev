# Best Heroku Free Alternatives for Developers

After November 28, 2022, Heroku's free tier for Dynos, Postgres, and Data for Redis will no longer be available. If you're looking for a free Heroku alternative, this list provides a variety of services with free plans for developers.

## Free Heroku Alternatives

### [render.com](https://render.com/)

One of the top Heroku alternatives with a free plan to get started. Render offers a generous free tier for deploying web services, static sites, databases, and more.

### [fly.io](https://fly.io/)

Run your full-stack apps (and databases!) all over the world with [Fly.io](https://fly.io/). No ops required. Their free plan includes generous resource limits for deploying globally distributed applications.

### [koyeb.com](https://www.koyeb.com)

[Koyeb](https://www.koyeb.com) is an excellent option for deploying full-stack apps and APIs globally. Enjoy high-end performance thanks to fully automated deployments to BareMetal servers and their built-in edge network.

### [flightcontrol.dev](https://www.flightcontrol.dev?ref=heroku-free-alternatives)

Easily deploy your full-stack, frontend, or backend apps on your own AWS account with [FlightControl](https://www.flightcontrol.dev?ref=heroku-free-alternatives). Free for individuals, with paid plans for teams and organizations.

### [railway.app](https://railway.app/)

[Railway](https://railway.app/) is a cloud platform that takes the complexity out of shipping software. Their free tier allows you to deploy and manage web services, databases, and more.

### [cyclic.sh](https://www.cyclic.sh/)

Connect your GitHub repo to [Cyclic](https://www.cyclic.sh/), and they'll build, deploy, and manage the hosting for you. Perfect for quickly deploying projects from your repositories.

### [qoddi.com](https://qoddi.com/)

[Qoddi](https://qoddi.com/) is a fully managed App Hosting Platform running on a tier 1 network at 10% of the cost of similar solutions. Offers a free tier for getting started.

### [deta.sh](https://www.deta.sh/)

Build & deploy your ideas on [Deta](https://www.deta.sh/), a developer-friendly cloud platform with a generous free tier for a variety of services, including web apps, databases, and more.

### [adaptable.io](https://adaptable.io/)

Simply connect your GitHub repository, and let [Adaptable](https://adaptable.io/) handle the rest. Adaptable offers a free tier for deploying web apps and managing infrastructure.

### [alwaysdata.com](https://www.alwaysdata.com/en/)

[AlwaysData](https://www.alwaysdata.com/en/) provides a free tier for hosting web applications, databases, and other services all in one place. Perfect for getting started with web development projects.

### [netlify.com](https://www.netlify.com/)

[Netlify](https://www.netlify.com/) offers free static site hosting with GitHub integration, making it a great option for deploying and hosting static websites and single-page applications.

### [withcoherence.com](https://www.withcoherence.com/)

[Coherence](https://www.withcoherence.com/) automates DevOps for early-stage teams, allowing you to focus on delivering customer value. Their free tier is perfect for getting started with modern DevOps practices.

### [northflank.com](https://www.northflank.com/)

With [NorthFlank](https://www.northflank.com/), you can deploy any code, job, or database in seconds. Their free tier is an excellent option for quickly deploying and testing applications or services.

### [cockroachlabs.cloud](https://cockroachlabs.cloud/)

[CockroachDB Serverless](https://cockroachlabs.cloud/) is a next-generation PostgreSQL database with truly elastic scaling and consumption-based pricing. It features a free tier, and no credit card is needed for signing up.

This list provides a wide range of free Heroku alternatives for developers to explore and find the best fit for their projects and needs. Feel free to submit a pull request or suggest additional services to improve the list further.
